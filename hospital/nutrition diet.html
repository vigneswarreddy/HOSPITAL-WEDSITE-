Healthy Means Cooking at Home

We believe that the first step to healthy eating begins simply with cooking at home. Knowing what goes into your food is the best way to get the most out of it.

Three tips to encourage healthy eating for children 2-8 years old:

1.Include a fruit and/or vegetable at every meal. Fresh, frozen, and canned produce are all good options.
2.Set a good example for children by eating a balanced diet.
3.Try combining new foods with preferred foods, using positive reinforcement, limiting the number of food choices, serving appropriate portion sizes, and serving consistent meals for picky eaters

Three tips to encourage healthy eating for 9-18 years old:

1. Provide healthy snacks that are low in fat, sodium, and added sugars. Examples include yogurt and fruit, crackers and tuna, and celery and peanut butter.
2. Encourage physical activity such as walking or playing team sports.
3. Promote healthy eating as a lifestyle, rather than focus on weight management, which may lead to disordered eating or issues with body image.
